Paradise Beach

Paradise Beach, also known as Plage Paradiso, is situated in Chunnambar, close to Pondicherry town. Adorned with the golden sand, this is a little-isolated beach. To reach here, you have to take a ferry across the backwaters, which takes around 20-30 minutes.

Entry Fees: 150/-

Entry Timing: 9 AM to 6 PM

Serenity Beach

The name “Serenity” is apt for the beach due to the tranquillity it exudes on all travellers visiting it. The calm and clean beach is a perfect getaway from the rush of the city. Moreover, the beach is less crowded, making it a top pick for honeymoon couples and families to relax and enjoy their 'Me' time.

Entry Fees: None

Entry Timing: 6 AM to 6 PM
