Madikeri Fort

Madikeri Fort was commissioned by the king Muddu Raja in the seventeenth century. On the occasion of announcing Madikeri as the new capital city of Coorg. The king also declared the construction of Madikeri Fort which was later captured by numerous rulers, including Tipu Sultan. The basic structure of this fort underwent numerous changes and the massive ones were made by the British during imperialism.

Entry Fees: Free

Entry Timing: 9 AM to 6:30 PM

Abbey Falls

If you are heading to Coorg on a road trip with your own vehicle it is not just easy to explore the places to visit on the way to Coorg but also all the amazing tourist sites within this beautiful destination. One such breathtaking place in Abbey Falls makes for one of the most popular places to see in Coorg. Located 1 km from the town of Madikeri, the waterfall is located between coffee plantations and spice estates.

Entry Fees: 15/-

Entry Timing: 9 AM to 5 PM

Abbey Falls Rd, Hebbettageri, Karnataka
