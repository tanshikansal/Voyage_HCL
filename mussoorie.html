Kempty Falls

Kempty Falls, surrounded by stunning mountain ranges, is one of Mussoorie's most beautiful attractions. Among the many Mussorie tourist attractions, it is without a doubt the most popular. Its elevation of 1,364 metres allows tourists to take in the magnificent views of the hill station and the natural splendours all around them.

Entry Fees: 120/-

Entry Timing: 10 AM to 6 PM

Laal Tibba

Mussoorie's tallest mountain, Lal Tibba, provides breathtaking vistas of the Himalayan range in all its splendour. Visitors can experience the pure beauty of the area thanks to the Japanese telescope that has been put here. The famous pilgrimages of Kedarnath and Badrinath are also located inside this mountain range. This peak is one of Mussoorie's most well-known destinations, because of its spectacular views and adjacent pilgrimage choices.

Entry Fees: 50/-

Entry Timing: 6 AM to 6 PM

Laal Tibba, Landour rd, Mussoorie, Uttarakhand
